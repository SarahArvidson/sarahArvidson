TACO TIME

TACO TIME is an acronym of my own invention! It stands for Take A Chance Often and Taste Its Mighty Effects.
TACO TIME is a tantalizing way to boil down "the aggregate of marginal gains initiated for self-advancemment through targeted engineering and entrepreneurship skills acquisition." At its core, it's just a self-improvement and progress mindset. I have a lot of enthusiasm and zest for life, and I want to get as much out of it as possible, so I'm usually trying to "level up" in several areas of my life at a time. The only way this is manageable for me is by conceptualizing my big, hairy audacious goals as projects with SMART goals baked in and served up with some good old-fashioned habit formation. The aggregate of marginal gains guarantees progress.
